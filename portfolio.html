MCENROE TUTORING & TRANSLATION CARD

My client, McEnroe Tutoring, wanted a business card that he could give to potential clients. He requested that the card meet the required specifications for Vistaprint and include his information. Aside from that I had free range on the design choices. I decided to use the colors red and blue due to their high contrast and the meanings they evoke. Blue is perceived as stable, while red is perceived as strong.

WORLD WILDLIFE FUND BROCHURE

For one of my Technical Communication classes I had to create a brochure for the World Wildlife Fund. The content is over the foundation and how the reader can help save the wildlife. Every picture was chosen with a goal in mind. The picture on the cover acts as a poster when the brochure is unfolded. The pictures on the left column are there to evoke emotion. Each of the animals eyes are centered as if they are looking at the reader for guidance.

UNT FRISCO MAP

Created deliverables that pertain new technologies found at UNT New College at Frisco. I was in charge of creating a map of the campus that showcases where each new technology is located. I used the blueprints of the building to recreate the map of the campus in Photoshop. I then created the icons and navigation pane to show what rooms had which technology. Finally, I used InDesign to create a foldable layout to make it more appealing to students.

BUS SAFETY BROCHURE

For one of my Technical Communication classes I had to fix a badly edited flyer. I used InDesign to create a bi-fold template where my information would go. Originally all the information was in one giant block of text with no real formatting. I organized the information into easy to find sections. One problem I found was the lack of representation of the Danger Zone. In order to fix that, I added an image showcasing the distance of the Danger Zone.
